Accessibility Statement

Welcome to the World of Rap, we are committed to providing a website that is accessible to all of our users, including those with disabilities. We aim to ensure that our website adheres to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Website Accessibility Features

Keyboard Navigation: Our website can be fully navigated using a keyboard, making it accessible to individuals who cannot use a mouse. Text Alternatives: All images on the website have descriptive alt text to provide information to users with visual impairments. Color Contrast: We have designed our website with high contrast colors to ensure that content is legible for users with visual impairments. Audio and Video: Audio and video content includes transcriptions and captions where applicable to accommodate users with hearing impairments.

Despite our best efforts, there may be content on our site that is not fully accessible. If you encounter any accessibility issues or need assistance, please contact us.
